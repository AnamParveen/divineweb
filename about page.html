Divine Educational Solutions, a computer teaching institute. The owner Mrs. Mariam Arkate follows the footsteps of her mother. Her mother was very keen to educate the girl. She said that girls should be well educated. As the up bringing of the child is totally on the mother rather than father. If the mother is well educated, she can help the child to cross all the hurdles in life.

A vision seen by the 3 main pillars of our family, Mr. Husain Arkate, (Late) Mrs. Halima Arkate and (Late) Mr. Nooruddin Arkate about computer literacy for the future generation. Back in 2000, a first step was initiated in rural areas in the name of Creative Technologies. The love with the native place, the initiative was taken. In the past 25 years, thousands of students have benefited with the computer skills in different sectors and successfully working.

As time passed, a new setup was initiated in Mumbai (Jogeshwari Area), as Divine Educational Solutions, with the computer literacy and skill development programme for the last 10 years. With computer literacy, it has been transformed to Digital College (A dream of Mr. Nooruddin Arkate to build a college).

The Arkate family holds a good place in society, with all the members of the family , engineers and doctors and other graduates. This family is always thinking about the uplift of education in society. If the people in the society are educated, this will boost the development of the nation.

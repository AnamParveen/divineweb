M.Com (Master of Commerce)

A Master of Commerce (MCom) is a two-year postgraduate degree that focuses on commerce, accounting, management, and economics. It's also known as a Magister Commercii (MagComm or Mag. comm.) or a Master of Science in Commerce (MS-Comm, MS-Com, MS-MComm, or MS-MCom).

Career options after M.Com:



⏵Chartered Accountant (CA)
⏵Company Secretary (CS)
⏵Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)
⏵Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
⏵Investment Banker
⏵Tax Advisor
